# εἰδωλεῖον/eidōleion
This word is a type of thing. It is a noun.

* This word means a house or temple that someone built for an idol. An idol is an object that people worship as a god.
    * The building could be small enough to hold only the idol.
    * The building could be big enough to also hold worshippers gathering together.

See: 1 Corinthians 8:10
