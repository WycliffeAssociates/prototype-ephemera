# προηγέομαι/proēgeomai

Sometimes this word describes a situation or how something is. It is a verb. However, it is not an action. 

* This word can mean to be eager in doing something.
    * This word is used to talk about a person who strongly desires to do something.

**Advice to translators**: This type of verb describes a state of being. 

Sometimes this word is an action. It is a verb.

* This word can mean to do something in a way that is much better than what people usually do.
* This word can mean to go before as a leader.
    * This word is used to talk about a person who lives in a way that shows other people the way that they should live.

See: Romans 12:10
