# δικαίωμα/dikaiōma
This word is a type of thing or idea. It is a noun.
* This word can mean a good action that a person does.
* This word can mean the action of one person saying or showing that another person has not done something evil.
* This word can mean the action of one person saying or showing that another person is good.
* This word can mean a good command.
* This word can mean the words of a judge who says that a person is guilty or innocent.
