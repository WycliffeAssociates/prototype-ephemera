# σαλπίζω/salpizō
This word is an action. It is a verb.
* This word can mean to blow a trumpet sound or to sound a trumpet. It talks about causing a trumpet to produce a sound. 
* This word can mean to make the sound of a trumpet. It talks about the sound coming from the trumpet.
* This word can mean to do something that is like causing a trumpet to produce a sound.
