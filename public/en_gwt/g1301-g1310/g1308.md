# διαφέρω/diapherō
Sometimes this word is an action. It is a verb.

* This word can mean to move or carry something through an area.

Sometimes this word describes a situation or how something is. It is a verb. However, it is not an action. 

* This word can mean to be worth more than something else. 

* This word can mean to be different from someone or something else.
    * Sometimes this word is used to talk about something being different because it is much better than something else.


**Advice to translators**: This type of verb describes a state of being.
