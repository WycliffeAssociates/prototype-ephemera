# ἀρχιερεύς/archiereus 
This word is a type of person. It is a noun. 

* This word can mean chief priest
    * The chief priests were important priests and leaders over other priests.
* This word can mean high pries
    * The high priest was the leader and most important of all the priests.
* This word can mean someone who is like a high priest in some way.
    * When this word is used in this way, it describes Jesus.
