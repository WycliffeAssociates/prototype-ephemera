# προσκόπτω/proskoptō
This word is an action. It is a verb.
* This word can mean to strike or beat  against something.
* This word can mean to stumble or fall over something.
* This word can mean to be angry at or insulted by another person's actions or words.
* This word can be used to talk about someone doing something that is somehow like stumbling or falling over something.
